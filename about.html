Who Am I?

I'm Salvador Ferman Sanchez, a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast currently pursuing my education in Computer Technology at Lone Star College. My journey in the world of technology began with a curiosity about how systems work and evolved into a dedication to protecting them.

With a strong foundation in IT fundamentals and specialized knowledge in cybersecurity, I'm constantly expanding my skills through hands-on projects, certifications, and continuous learning. My goal is to contribute to creating safer digital environments and helping organizations protect their valuable assets.

My Approach

I believe in a proactive approach to cybersecurity, focusing on prevention rather than just reaction. My methodology combines technical expertise with an understanding of human factors in security, creating comprehensive protection strategies.

Personal Interests

Beyond cybersecurity, I enjoy staying updated with the latest technology trends, participating in CTF competitions, and contributing to open-source security projects. In my free time, I enjoy hiking, reading about emerging technologies, and mentoring others interested in cybersecurity.
